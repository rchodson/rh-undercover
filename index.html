Shhh!

Don’t tell: I’m living a double life. My neighbors think I’m your basic suburbanite minivan mom, schlepping kids to school and soccer and music lessons, maybe eating bon bons in between, my biggest secret the store-bought brownies I tried to pass off as my own at the school bake sale.

But no.

I mean, yeah, sure. I am that.

But also I’m the woman at a crossroad in her life, discovering new passions and looking for ways to improve her prospects for fulfillment and financial independence. I’m the mom of daughters plotting ways to model their own future self-sufficiency. I’m the internet addict who wants in on the action.

I’m amassing new skills — learning to code, learning principles of design and usability — and hatching schemes for a self-directed career in tech. I've got a secret plan to transform my circumstances.

Who knows? Today my life; maybe tomorrow, the world!
